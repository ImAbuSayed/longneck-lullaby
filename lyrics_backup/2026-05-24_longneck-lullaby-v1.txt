[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The midnight hour is closing in, just you and me
Got a cold longneck and a perfect memory
I want to pull you close and let the world fade out
You are the only thing my mind is thinking about

[Verse 1]
The neon sign is buzzing down the empty street
I trace the shadows falling right across your feet
Your fingertips are warm against my calloused hand
We found a quiet corner in this noisy land

[Pre-Chorus]
The ice is melting in my glass, but I don't care
I am completely tangled in your messy hair
The tension building up is tearing down my walls
I am ready for wherever this sweet evening falls

[Chorus]
This is our longneck lullaby, a slow-burn kind of ride
I want to keep you right here by my side
With every single breath, you pull me further in
Let the music stop so we can finally begin

[Post-Chorus]
(Pull me further in, pull me further in)
We are riding on this longneck lullaby

[Verse 2]
You give a little smile that gives away the game
It always drives me crazy when you say my name
I feel the chemistry just pouring from your eyes
It is the kind of magic that you can't disguise

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

I want to keep you right here by my side

[Post-Chorus 2]
(Pull me further in, pull me further in)
We are riding on this longneck lullaby

[Bridge]
We are fading into silence while the fire glows
This is the kind of destiny the shadow knows
No matter where we run, the echo pulls us near
The light is fading out but everything is clear

[Spoken]
I feel the rhythm of your beating heart within
Say my name softly, Saeed tonight (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Final Chorus]
